Design Like Mad 2014 Schedule

Design Like Mad is Saturday, September 27th from 9am-9pm at the Madison Public Library: Central Library. The following are more details about the event.

Design Like Mad Schedule of Events

8:30am - 9:00am: Check-In and Breakfast (supplied by Willy Street Co-Op)

9:00am: Kickoff

11:40am - 12:00pm: Non-Profits: First Feedback Session

12:00pm - Lunch Served (supplied by Milios)

3:00pm - 3:20pm: Non-Profits: Second Feedback Session

6:00pm: Dinner Served (supplied by Ian's Pizza)

7:00pm - 8:00pm: Finalize Products, Print Materials, and Powerpoints Due

7:30pm - 9:00pm: Drinks (supplied by Forequarter)

8:00pm - 9:00pm: Final Presentations

Check-in

Check-in will be 8:30-9:00am at the Madison Public Library (201 W Mifflin St Madison, WI 53703) in the Third Floor Conference Room. Volunteers will be around to guide you to the appropriate location.

Supplies

Here's a list of supplies you may want to bring:

  • Computer with all necessary software (if you need a laptop and have not been contacted by Abby, please email her at abby@designlikemad.org to reserve a library laptop)
  • Computer Charger
  • USB Drive (or some way of transferring files)
  • Pencils/pens/any other writing materials/paper you may need for sketching
  • A computer mouse
Supplies

Here's a list of supplies you will have access to:
  • Library Printers
  • Library Computers
  • Exacto Knives and cutting mats
Public and Non-Profit Visitors

The event is free and open to the public on the day of the event during the library’s typical hours of 9am-5pm. Non-profit organizations are welcome to come in and give feedback to the designers during the design process. We have designated slots from 11:40am - 12:00pm and 3pm - 3:20pm for representatives from the non-profit organizations to stop by and check-in on the design process, answer questions, and give feedback to the designers. Non profits are also required to be at the final presentations beginning at 8pm.

Final Products

Final products are to be transferred to the non-profit organization at the conclusion of the event at 9:00pm. Teams are responsible for communicating with their non-profit organization about how the final products will be transferred and if additional training is necessary for the non-profits to navigate their new products.

Release Agreements

Upon arrival, you will receive a Work Release Agreement and Talent Release Agreement to sign. Volunteers will be there to answer any questions you may have.

Final Presentations

After a long day of hard work relax and have some drinks supplied by 4-Quartar. Non profit coordinators and members will join us to celebrate the accomplishments of all the designers.

Questions

If you have any additional questions regarding the event please contact Abby (abby@designlikemad.org)
